# Enable Propagation Conditions

`View > Propagation Conditions` is a checkable menu item that toggles a real-time ionospheric propagation forecast overlay on your panadapters. Enable it to see current band condition data without leaving the main display.

## Before you start

- AetherSDR must be running and connected to a FLEX-8600 radio.
- Your system must have network access to retrieve propagation forecast data.

## Steps

1. Click `View` in the menu bar.
2. Click `Propagation Conditions` to check or uncheck it.

When checked, the propagation forecast overlay appears on your panadapters. Click the item again to remove the overlay.
