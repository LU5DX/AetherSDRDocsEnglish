# Enable Propagation Conditions

`View > Propagation Conditions` is a checkable menu item that toggles a real-time ionospheric propagation forecast overlay on your panadapters. Enable it to see current band condition data without leaving the main AetherSDR window.

## Before you start

- AetherSDR must be running and connected to a Flex radio.
- At least one panadapter must be open and visible.

## Steps

1. Click `View` in the menu bar.
2. Click `Propagation Conditions`.

A check mark appears next to the item when the overlay is active. The propagation forecast overlay appears on your panadapters. Click `Propagation Conditions` again to remove the check mark and hide the overlay.
