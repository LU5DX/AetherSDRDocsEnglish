# Close an extra panadapter

When you have multiple panadapters open in multi-slice mode, you can close any one you no longer need. The × button on a panadapter's title bar sends a close request to the radio and removes that panadapter from the layout.

## Before you start

- Your radio must be connected. The close button is not available when there is no radio connection.
- You must have more than one panadapter open. The × (close) button is hidden in single-pan mode.

## Steps

1. Locate the title bar at the top of the panadapter you want to close. It shows the slice name (for example, "Slice B") and a row of small buttons on the right.
2. Click ×.
3. The panadapter closes and is removed from the layout. The remaining panadapters resize to fill the space.

## Tips

- If you cannot see the × button, you are in single-pan mode — there is only one panadapter open and it cannot be closed this way.
- If the panadapter has been popped out into a floating window, its title bar still contains the × button. Click it there to close the floating panadapter.
