# Close an extra panadapter

When you have more than one panadapter open in multi-slice mode, you can close any extra one to reclaim screen space. The × button in each panadapter's title bar removes that panadapter from the layout.

## Before you start

- AetherSDR must be connected to the radio. The close button is only available when a radio connection is active.
- You must have at least two panadapters open. The × button is hidden in single-pan mode.

## Steps

1. Locate the title bar at the top of the panadapter you want to close. It shows the slice name (for example, "Slice B") and a row of small buttons on the right.
2. Click the × button on the right side of that title bar.
3. The panadapter closes and the remaining panadapters adjust to fill the available space.

## Tips

- If you do not see the × button, you are in single-pan mode. Only one panadapter is open, so there is nothing to close.
- To reduce a panadapter to a single-pan view without removing it entirely, use □ to maximize one panadapter instead. See [Maximize one panadapter to fill the main area](maximize-one-panadapter-to-fill-the-main-area.md).
