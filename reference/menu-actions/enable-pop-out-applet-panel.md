# Enable Pop Out Applet Panel

`View > Pop Out Applet Panel` floats the applet panel into its own separate window, or docks it back into the main AetherSDR window. Use this when you want to move the applet panel to a second monitor or reposition it independently of the main window.

## Before you start

- The applet panel must be visible. If it is hidden, enable it first via `View > Applet Panel`.

## Steps

1. Click `View` in the menu bar.
2. Click `Pop Out Applet Panel`.
   The applet panel detaches from the main window and appears as a separate floating window.
3. To dock it back, click `View > Pop Out Applet Panel` again.
   The checkmark is removed and the panel returns to its docked position.

## Tips

- The keyboard shortcut `Ctrl+Shift+S` toggles pop-out without opening the menu.
- When floating, the applet panel window can be moved and resized independently. Drag it to a second monitor if needed.
